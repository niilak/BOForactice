Created and curated for fashion professionals

As the global fashion industry adapts to social, economic and technological changes - whilst striving to be better for people and the planet - BoF Professional membership provides the insight, analysis and advice you need to navigate what lies ahead for the industry, and your own career.

Through our global team of journalists and editors, in conjunction with our wider network of leading fashion creatives, executives and innovators, BoF Professional members enjoy exclusive access to the very latest thinking on Luxury, Retail, Beauty, Sustainability, Technology, Media, Marketing & PR, Workplace & Talent, Entrepreneurship and Global Markets.

With content created and curated specifically for fashion professionals, BoF Professional connects you to the fast-moving fashion industry through unrivalled world-class journalism, in-depth case studies, on-demand masterclasses and unmissable digital live events.

All-Access Membership Includes:

BoF Professional membership includes:

Daily Analysis and Advice

Stay one step ahead with unrivalled, world-class journalism covering: Luxury, Retail, Sustainability, Beauty, Technology, Media, Marketing & PR, Workplace & Talent, Entrepreneurship and Global Markets.

Essential Email Briefings

Ensure you are always well informed on the most important fashion industry developments with our must-read weekly, members-only newsletters: This Week in Fashion, The Week Ahead and China Decoded

BoF Professional iPhone App

Catch-up on BoF Professional on the go via our newly updated BoF Professional iPhone app,with daily newsfeed, extensive archive and topic-based navigation in an ad-free environment exclusively for BoF Professional members.

Case Studies

Dive deep on key business opportunities, with our monthly case studies covering trending topics across fashion marketing, retail, technology and e-commerce. Recent case studies include The New 4Ps of DTC Marketing, Fashion's Last Mile Opportunity and How to Sell Skincare at Every Price.

Online Courses

Discover watch-on-demand masterclasses and courses to drive continuous learning and development, including The BoF Sustainability Masterclass Series, The Art and Science of Buying and Merchandising and Fashion History for Today..

Live Events

Find inspiration and connect with the BoF Professional community through our regular program of unmissable digital live events, including our weekly #BOFLive Series, Sustainability Summit and VOICES, our annual gathering for big thinkers. If you can't join us live, all events are also available to watch-on-demand.
